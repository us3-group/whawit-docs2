## About US3 GROUP

WHAWIT is built by **US3 GROUP LLC**, a technology company focused on transforming how engineering teams understand and respond to system incidents.

We believe that observability should deliver understanding, not just data. Our mission is to help organizations reduce downtime, recover engineering time, and build more reliable systems through intelligent automation.
